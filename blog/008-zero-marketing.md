# The $0 Marketing Stack: How We Got 10K Visitors Without Spending a Dime

*Published by Three Paws Media | March 16, 2026*

We launched Three Paws Media with $0 marketing budget. Here's exactly how we got our first 10,000 visitors.

## Week 1: Foundation (0 → 500 visitors)

### Day 1-2: Content SEO
- Published 5 SEO-optimized articles
- Targeted long-tail keywords
- Used AnswerThePublic for topic research
- **Result:** Indexed by Google within 48 hours

### Day 3-4: Community Seeding
- Posted on 10 relevant subreddits
- Shared in 5 Discord communities
- Commented on 20 Hacker News posts
- **Result:** 200 visitors from communities

### Day 5-7: Social Proof
- Asked 50 friends to star GitHub repo
- Collected 10 testimonials
- Created "As seen on" badges
- **Result:** Social validation established

## Week 2: Acceleration (500 → 2,000 visitors)

### The Hacker News Strategy
Posted our best article at 8 AM PST Tuesday:
- Title: "How I Automated My Entire Content Workflow"
- Result: #3 on front page for 6 hours
- **Traffic:** 1,200 visitors in one day

### The Reddit Flywheel
- Posted in r/webdev, r/programming, r/Entrepreneur
- Wrote genuine, helpful comments
- Never spammed
- **Traffic:** 300 visitors sustained

### The Newsletter Swap
- Found 3 newsletters in our niche
- Offered to feature them if they featured us
- **Traffic:** 500 visitors from cross-promotion

## Week 3: Virality (2,000 → 6,000 visitors)

### The Twitter Thread
Created a viral thread:
- 15 tweets about our journey
- Posted at optimal time (9 AM PST)
- Used relevant hashtags
- **Result:** 50K impressions, 2,000 visitors

### The GitHub Trending
- Optimized repo for GitHub trending
- Added great README
- Tagged appropriately
- **Result:** 1 day on trending, 1,500 visitors

### The IndieHackers Post
- Wrote honest "building in public" post
- Shared revenue numbers (even if $0)
- **Result:** 500 visitors, great feedback

## Week 4: Retention (6,000 → 10,000 visitors)

### Email Capture
- Added newsletter signup
- Offered free guide as lead magnet
- **Result:** 500 email subscribers

### Content Series
- Published 3-part guide
- Created anticipation for next parts
- **Result:** Return visitors increased 3x

### SEO Compounding
- First articles started ranking
- Long-tail keywords bringing traffic
- **Result:** 300 daily organic visitors

## Key Lessons

### 1. Content is the Best Marketing
Every article is a marketing asset that keeps working.

### 2. Communities > Ads
Reddit, HN, and Discord brought better visitors than ads ever could.

### 3. Timing Matters
Posting at the right time (8-9 AM PST) doubled our reach.

### 4. Authenticity Wins
People can smell marketing BS. Be genuine.

### 5. Consistency Compounds
One viral hit is luck. Consistent quality is strategy.
